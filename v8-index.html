Alone


From childhood's hour I have not been

As others were I have not seen As others saw I could not bring My passions from a common spring From the same source I have not taken My sorrow I could not awaken My heart to joy at the same tone

And all I lov'd I lov'd

alone

Then in my childhood in the dawn Of a most stormy life was drawn From ev'ry depth of good and ill The mystery which binds me still From the torrent, or the fountain From the red cliff of the mountain From the sun that 'round me roll'd
